**AXIS_SET_ADMISSIBILITY_v1**  
  
**[ASSUME] ASA01 DEPENDENCY: This specification presupposes frozen axis-like function admissibility for individual function relations F_i(x,v), and frozen carrier/transport/obstruction structure.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA02 AXIS_SET_SYMBOL_SCHEMA: A finite axis-set is admissible only as an explicitly declared token Σ together with a finite declared membership relation Mem(Σ,F) where each member F is an admitted axis-like function relation symbol.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA03 AXIS_SET_SCOPE_RESTRICTION: No admissible axis-set Σ may contain any symbol that is not an admitted axis-like function relation symbol.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA04 NONCOORDINATE_GUARANTEE: No admissible axis-set may assert that Σ determines a coordinate system, frame, basis, or global parameterization of carrier tokens or state tokens.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA05 NO_RANK_OR_COMPLETENESS: No admissible axis-set may assert any rank, completeness, spanning, or exhaustiveness property of Σ relative to the carrier or any family of relations.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA06 NO_INDEPENDENCE_ASSUMPTION: No admissible axis-set may assume linear independence, orthogonality, functional independence, or non-redundancy among its members.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA07 NO_CLOSURE_OR_SPAN: No admissible axis-set may assert closure of Σ under any combination rule, nor assert that Σ generates or spans any larger class of functions.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA08 TRANSPORT_ORDER_SENSITIVITY_PRESERVATION: No admissible axis-set may include any schema that forces transport composition order-sensitivity witnesses to vanish or become indistinguishable solely due to joint use of members of Σ.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA09 OBSTRUCTION_PRESERVATION: No admissible axis-set may include any schema that forces obstruction witnesses to vanish or become indistinguishable solely due to joint use of members of Σ.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA10 PARTIALITY_DEFAULT: No admissible axis-set may assume all its member functions are defined for all carrier tokens or all state tokens.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA11 NO_GLOBAL_AXIS_SET: No admissible axis-set may assume existence of a single axis-set Σ that applies globally to all compatibility components or all declared admissible domains.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA12 FORBIDDEN_INDUCED_ORDER: No admissible axis-set may introduce any schema that induces a global order on carrier tokens or state tokens by aggregating values across members of Σ.**  
  
**[DERIVE] ASA13 FORBIDDEN_VECTOR_SCHEMA: No admissible axis-set may assert that tuples of values across members of Σ form vectors, coordinates, or elements of any structured product space with substitution power.**  
  
**[OPEN] ASA14 COORDINATE_FRAME_EXTENSIONS: Admissibility of promoting certain axis-sets into coordinate frames or bases is open, provided this remains removable and introduces no substitution power.**  
  
**[OPEN] ASA15 ORTHOGONALITY_EXTENSIONS: Admissibility of orthogonality-like or independence-like relations among members of Σ is open, provided no metric, rank, or completeness primitives are introduced.**  
  
**[OPEN] ASA16 COMPLETENESS_EXTENSIONS: Admissibility of completeness or spanning-like notions for Σ is open, provided no global parameterization or coordinate semantics is asserted by default.**
