**COMPLETENESS_ADMISSIBILITY_v1**  
  
**[ASSUME] CPA01 DEPENDENCY: This specification presupposes admitted finite axis-sets Σ with membership Mem(Σ,F), admitted axis-like functions, and frozen carrier/transport/obstruction structure.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA02 COMPLETENESS_SYMBOL_SCHEMA: A completeness- or spanning-like claim is admissible only as an explicitly declared unary relation symbol Comp(Σ) ranging over admitted axis-set tokens Σ.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA03 COMPLETENESS_PARTIALITY_DEFAULT: No admissible completeness-like claim may assume Comp(Σ) holds for all admitted axis-sets, nor that Comp(Σ) is decidable or witnessed for any Σ by default.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA04 NONBASIS_GUARANTEE: No admissible completeness-like claim may assert or imply that any Σ satisfying Comp forms a basis, coordinate frame, or coordinate system for carrier or state tokens.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA05 NORANK_NODIMENSION_GUARANTEE: No admissible completeness-like claim may assert or imply rank, dimensionality, minimal cardinality, or uniqueness of any Σ satisfying Comp.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA06 NO_IDENTITY_FROM_COMPLETENESS: No admissible completeness-like claim may assert that Comp(Σ) implies identity, equality, or substitutability of any carrier tokens, state tokens, or function symbols.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA07 NO_SUBSTITUTION_FROM_COMPLETENESS: No admissible completeness-like claim may introduce any schema permitting replacement of any token or function symbol on the basis of Comp.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA08 TRANSPORT_ORDER_SENSITIVITY_PRESERVATION: No admissible completeness-like claim may include any schema that forces transport composition order-sensitivity witnesses to vanish or become indistinguishable solely due to Comp declarations.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA09 OBSTRUCTION_PRESERVATION: No admissible completeness-like claim may include any schema that forces obstruction witnesses to vanish or become indistinguishable solely due to Comp declarations.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA10 NO_GLOBAL_COMPLETENESS: No admissible completeness-like claim may assert existence of a global canonical axis-set Σ* such that Comp(Σ*) and Σ* applies across all compatibility components or admissible domains.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA11 FORBIDDEN_SPAN_SCHEMA: No admissible completeness-like claim may include any universal schema asserting that Comp(Σ) implies every admissible axis-like function is generated, derived, or representable by members of Σ.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA12 FORBIDDEN_UNIQUENESS_SCHEMA: No admissible completeness-like claim may include any universal schema asserting uniqueness or equivalence of any two axis-sets satisfying Comp.**  
  
**[DERIVE] CPA13 FORBIDDEN_ORTHOGONAL_COMPLETENESS_SCHEMA: No admissible completeness-like claim may include any schema asserting that Orth relations plus Comp imply basis-like structure, orthonormality, or coordinate representation.**  
  
**[OPEN] CPA14 BASIS_EXTENSIONS: Admissibility of promoting certain Comp(Σ) claims into basis or frame notions is open, provided these remain removable overlays and introduce no substitution power.**  
  
**[OPEN] CPA15 SPANNING_SYSTEM_EXTENSIONS: Admissibility of spanning-like systems that introduce representability constraints is open, provided no rank, dimensionality, or global coordinate semantics is asserted by default.**  
  
**[OPEN] CPA16 HILBERT_COMPLETENESS_EXTENSIONS: Admissibility of Hilbert-style completeness notions is open, provided they do not bind kernel admissibility and do not assume completed infinities.**
