**DUALITY_CLASS_ADMISSIBILITY_v1**  
  
**[ASSUME] DCA01 DEPENDENCY: This specification presupposes frozen carrier/state token admission, transport and obstruction instances, and optional axis-function and axis-set structures, without assuming any total classification on any domain.**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA02 DUALITY_SYMBOL_SCHEMA: A duality-like structure is admissible only as an explicitly declared binary relation symbol Dual(x,y) or an explicitly declared classifier relation Class2(x,c) where x ranges over admitted carrier tokens, state tokens, transport instances, or obstruction instances, and c ranges over a finite declared two-class token registry C2.**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA03 DUALITY_PARTIALITY_DEFAULT: No admissible duality-like structure may assume Dual(x,y) or Class2(x,c) is defined for all admissible x, nor that every x belongs to exactly one class token in C2.**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA04 NONONTOLOGY_GUARANTEE: No admissible duality-like structure may assert or imply two kinds of being, substance, or necessity; duality is classification of admitted tokens only.**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA05 NONAXIS_NONCOORDINATE_GUARANTEE: No admissible duality-like structure may assert or imply axes, coordinate frames, bases, rank, dimensionality, or any global parameterization of the carrier.**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA06 NO_IDENTITY_FROM_DUALITY: No admissible duality-like structure may assert that duality or class membership implies identity, equality, or indistinguishability of tokens or relation instances.**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA07 NO_SUBSTITUTION_FROM_DUALITY: No admissible duality-like structure may introduce any schema permitting replacement of any token or relation instance on the basis of Dual or Class2 membership.**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA08 TRANSPORT_ORDER_SENSITIVITY_PRESERVATION: No admissible duality-like structure may include any schema that forces transport composition order-sensitivity witnesses to vanish or become indistinguishable solely due to duality declarations.**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA09 OBSTRUCTION_PRESERVATION: No admissible duality-like structure may include any schema that forces obstruction witnesses to vanish or become indistinguishable solely due to duality declarations.**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA10 NO_GLOBAL_PARTITION_SCHEMA: No admissible duality-like structure may include any universal schema asserting a mandatory two-class partition of all admissible objects in any declared domain.**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA11 FORBIDDEN_CLASS_EXHAUSTIVENESS_SCHEMA: No admissible duality-like structure may assert that for every x there exists c in C2 such that Class2(x,c).**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA12 FORBIDDEN_CLASS_UNIQUENESS_SCHEMA: No admissible duality-like structure may assert that for every x there exists exactly one c in C2 such that Class2(x,c).**  
  
**[DERIVE] DCA13 FORBIDDEN_INVOLUTION_SCHEMA: No admissible duality-like structure may assert existence of a universal involution map Inv(x) or equivalence-like pairing schema derived from Dual by default.**  
  
**[OPEN] DCA14 INVOLUTION_EXTENSIONS: Admissibility of involution-like maps is open, provided they introduce no identity, equality, substitution power, or global totality by default.**  
  
**[OPEN] DCA15 CONJUGATION_EXTENSIONS: Admissibility of conjugation-like pairings is open, provided they remain structural classifications and do not induce coordinate or metric structure.**  
  
**[OPEN] DCA16 SYMMETRY_EXTENSIONS: Admissibility of symmetry-like notions relating duality classes is open, provided no ontological necessity or global partition is asserted.**
