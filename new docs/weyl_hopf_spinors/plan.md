# Left and Right Weyl Spinors on Nested Hopf Tori

## Overview
- Topic: a pre-Axis-consistent visualization of left- and right-handed Weyl spinors moving on nested Hopf tori
- Hook: "Why do the two chiral branches need geometry at all?"
- Aha moment: the left/right Weyl split is not just algebraic sign bookkeeping; it is a geometric pairing on admissible toroidal structure
- Target audience: technical readers familiar with spinors, chirality, and the pre-Axis ladder
- Length: ~45–60 seconds
- Resolution: 480p draft, 1080p final
