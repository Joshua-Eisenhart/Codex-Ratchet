- preserved:
  - local SIM_ID and evidence contracts are explicit
  - repo-top catalog and evidence-pack visibility remain zero
- not collapsed into:

3. Shared lattice vs changed contract
- preserved:
  - `v1` and `v2` share the same axis12 lattice and broad producer role
  - their storage contracts, control families, and `num_states` differ materially
- not collapsed into:
  - "v2 is just a renamed or lightly hardened v1"

4. Versioned result sinks vs unversioned evidence sink
- preserved:
  - versioned result filenames do not collide
  - `sim_evidence_pack.txt` does collide
- not collapsed into:
  - "both versions can coexist cleanly without path management"

5. Producer strip vs next orphan passes
- preserved:
  - the result-only orphan triplets are generated by this strip
  - they still remain separate source classes for the next passes
- not collapsed into:
  - "the current runner-only batch already covers the result surfaces"
